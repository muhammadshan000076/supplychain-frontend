About Our Decentralized Supply Chain Management System

Our Mission

To revolutionize supply chain transparency and accountability by leveraging blockchain technology and decentralized systems. We aim to eliminate counterfeit products, reduce fraud, and build trust among all stakeholders in the supply chain ecosystem.

What We Do

Our Decentralized Supply Chain Management System provides a secure, transparent, and immutable record of product movement from manufacturer to customer. Using blockchain technology and unique product identifiers (QR codes), we ensure authenticity and enable real-time tracking of products throughout their journey.

Key Features

  • User Authentication: Secure login with role-based access control
  • Product Registration: Manufacturers register products with unique IDs and QR codes
  • Real-Time Tracking: Monitor product movement at every stage
  • Blockchain Recording: Immutable and transparent audit trails
  • Product Verification: Customers verify authenticity using QR codes
  • Supply Chain Status Updates: Distributors and retailers update product locations and status
  • Report Management: Comprehensive analytics for decision-making
  • Decentralized Architecture: No single point of failure or control

User Roles

  • Manufacturer: Registers new products and initiates supply chain tracking
  • Distributor: Updates product status and location during distribution
  • Retailer: Updates product information at retail points
  • Customer: Verifies product authenticity and views product history
  • Admin: Manages system operations, users, and generates reports

Security & Trust

Every transaction and update is securely recorded on the blockchain, ensuring data immutability and transparency. Our system uses industry-standard encryption protocols (HTTPS) and password hashing to protect sensitive information. Role-based access control ensures that only authorized users can perform specific operations, maintaining system integrity.

Benefits

  • Eliminates counterfeit products from the supply chain
  • Reduces fraud and unauthorized modifications
  • Improves supply chain visibility and efficiency
  • Builds customer trust through transparency
  • Enables quick identification of supply chain issues
  • Provides complete audit trails for compliance
  • Reduces operational delays and errors
  • Supports informed decision-making through analytics

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+)
  • Authentication & Database: SQL(PostgreSQL)
  • Blockchain Integration: Smart contract support for immutable records
  • QR Code Generation: Dynamic QR code generation for product tracking
  • Deployment: Netlify (Static hosting)
